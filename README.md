Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis  

Tools Used: MS Excel,Pivot table, Pivot Charts, Data Visualization

Project Overview: This project analyzes the sales data of Vrinda Store . The goal is to derive actionable insights from the dataset and provide recommendations to improve sales by identifying top-performing customer segments, regions, and sales channels.

Dataset : The dataset contains monthly sales data including:
1) Order ID, Amount, Customer Gender, Age Group, State, Product Category, and Sales Channel.
2) Data cleaning and validation were performed to ensure accuracy and completeness.

Workflow:
1) Data Collection: Collected sales data from Vrinda Store’s database.
2) Data Cleaning: Checked for null values and corrected inconsistencies.
3) Data Processing: Identified relationships between columns where necessary to support reporting.
4) Data Analysis & Visualization: Created charts and applied user requirements for better understanding of trends.

Key Insights:
1) Gender: Women contribute to ~65% of total sales.
2) Top States: Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the leading states in sales.
3) Age Group: Adults (30-49 years) account for ~50% of total purchases.
4) Sales Channels: Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the top-performing channels.

Recommendations:
1)Focus marketing campaigns and offers on women aged 30-49.
2)Prioritize promotions and inventory for Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.
3) Strengthen presence and targeted offers on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.

Visualizations:
1) Sales vs Orders: Monthly sales trend and order count.
2) Sales by Gender: Comparison of male vs female purchases.
3) Order Status: Delivered, Cancelled, Refunded, Returned.
4) Top 5 States: States contributing highest sales.
5) Orders by Age vs Gender: Distribution of purchases across age groups.
6) Orders by Channel: Contribution of each sales channel.
7) Charts generated using Excel for better visualization and decision-making.



Visualization: Excel Charts

Conclusion

By targeting the right customer segment (women aged 30–49) in high-performing states through top channels, Vrinda Store can maximize sales and optimize marketing efforts.
