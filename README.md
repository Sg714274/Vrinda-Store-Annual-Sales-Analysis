Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis 
Project Overview

This project analyzes the sales data of Vrinda Store . The goal is to derive actionable insights from the dataset and provide recommendations to improve sales by identifying top-performing customer segments, regions, and sales channels.

Dataset

The dataset contains monthly sales data including:

Order ID, Amount, Customer Gender, Age Group, State, Product Category, and Sales Channel.

Data cleaning and validation were performed to ensure accuracy and completeness.

Workflow

Data Collection: Collected sales data from Vrinda Store’s database.

Data Cleaning: Checked for null values and corrected inconsistencies.

Data Processing: Identified relationships between columns where necessary to support reporting.

Data Analysis & Visualization: Created charts and applied user requirements for better understanding of trends.

Key Insights

Gender: Women contribute to ~65% of total sales.

Top States: Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh are the leading states in sales.

Age Group: Adults (30-49 years) account for ~50% of total purchases.

Sales Channels: Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra are the top-performing channels.

Recommendations

Focus marketing campaigns and offers on women aged 30-49.

Prioritize promotions and inventory for Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.

Strengthen presence and targeted offers on Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.

Visualizations

Sales vs Orders: Monthly sales trend and order count.

Sales by Gender: Comparison of male vs female purchases.

Order Status: Delivered, Cancelled, Refunded, Returned.

Top 5 States: States contributing highest sales.

Orders by Age vs Gender: Distribution of purchases across age groups.

Orders by Channel: Contribution of each sales channel.

Charts generated using Excel for better visualization and decision-making.

Tools Used

Data Cleaning & Analysis: MS Excel, Power Query

Visualization: Excel Charts

Conclusion

By targeting the right customer segment (women aged 30–49) in high-performing states through top channels, Vrinda Store can maximize sales and optimize marketing efforts.
